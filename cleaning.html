KEEPING OUR GYM SAFE & SQUEAKY CLEAN

Our members are our top priority! That’s why we’re following super -strict safety and cleaning protocols.So you can gym confidently knowing your gyms is squeaky clean.

WELCOME TO THE FITCOON GYMS

TEAM MEMBERS IN MASKS

All PF Team Members are required to wear masks at all times while in-club.

TOUCHLESS CHECK-IN

Avoid unnecessary contact by checking in using your digital ID card for check-in.

CLEANING PLATFORM

Each cleaning station throughout the club will be stocked with paper towels and disinfectant spray effective against COVID-19. Hand sanitizer stations will also be replenished regularly.

CARDIO DISTANCING

Some cardio and strength machines are not available for use to give our members a little extra breathing room.

PLEASE BE CLEANSIDERATE

Our members play an important role in helping us keep our clubs clean and safe. So, here are some ways you can Be Cleansiderate.

DON’T FORGET YOUR MASK!

We’re requiring all members to wear masks while in-GYMS so you can workout safely and confidently.

SCRUB UP BEFORE & AFTER WORKING OUT

Wash your hands for 20 seconds before and after using the equipment.

PRACTICE SOCIAL FITNESSING

Keep a safe distance by putting an imaginary treadmill, or two, between you and others.

NOT FEELING GREAT?

Take a rest day or try a workout at home

HAVE FUN WITH SAFE ENVIRONMENT

Give encouragement without touching
